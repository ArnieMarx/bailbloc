About Bail Bloc

Bail Bloc is a computer program that bails people out of jail.

The app is completely safe and private, and allows you to keep working as normal.

Bail Bloc uses a small portion of your computer's processing power to mine Monero, a relatively new cryptocurrency that has strong privacy features and is much easier and faster to mine than BitCoin or Ethereum.

All earnings go into a single wallet controlled by the Bronx Freedom Fund, a nonprofit revolving bail fund based in the South Bronx. At the end of every month, the earned Monero are exchanged for US dollars and contributed directly to the bail fund.

Most users can expect to generate around $3 to $5 per month. Individually it isn't much, but it adds up quickly. And, because bail funds roll over, even a small contribution can have a lasting effect.

The code is open source, and we welcome all contributions.
